< Personal Thought and Websites about Global Warming >

Hello, stranger! Thank you for revisiting my blog.
Today I brought, up an important topic we should think about, Global Warming.

Have you ever thought about how many aspects of nature and human life have been changed in bad ways? It's uncountable. Every year, every part of the world has suffered from global warming, which is getting worse. For example, rising temperatures, melting ice leading to higher sea levels, corruption of the ecosystem, etc. In addition, according to CCAC, the climate getting warmer finally raises the possibility of having diseases and illnesses(Climate and Clean Air Coalition, Why we need to act now.). It means that not only nature but also we are not safe from global warming. This is why we have to think about this topic seriously.

    Following are some lists of websites related to global warming.
    If you want a deeper journey to global warming, visit there and find out what has been discussed to solve the problem.

  • The Climate Reality Project is a famous community for its dedication to promoting people's awareness of global warming and climate change. On this page, you can find what they think is the reason for climate change, and what they have done to solve the problem.
  • National Geographic – Climate Change is well known for photography, you can find lists of amazing photos showing the cruel reality of climate change. If you want to know it by seeing it, National Geographic – Climate Change would be great.
  • Skeptical Science provides presents a skeptical view of misunderstandings of global warming denial and this page presents its own application to approach the sources easily.
  • 350.org is a great webpage to find strong demands to save our earth from the climate crisis. There is a map that shows their ongoing campaigns at the bottom of the page.
  • IPCC(Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change) provides as interesting well-structured reports about global warming. You can find and learn knowledge about climate change on this webpage.
  • WRI(World Resources Institute) – Climate gives us various data not only about climate but also about water, energy, etc. There are many sources to study and learn from.
